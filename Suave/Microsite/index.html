About Mary Alice Stephenson

Mary Alice Stephenson is a world renowned style and beauty expert, humanitarian and the founder and force behind GLAM4GOOD. Inspired by her tenure as National Fashion Ambassador for Make-A-Wish®, she launched GLAM4GOOD in 2012. Through GLAM4GOOD's partnerships with non-profits, beauty and fashion brands, she has been able to provide life changing makeovers, dramatic giveaways and transformative fashion experiences for everyday heroes and people in need.

GLAM4GOOD is on a mission to ignite positive change through style and transform lives through confidence-boosting makeovers, pampering the people who need it most. Check out the photos from past events and find one near you!

Upcoming Events

April 2014 • Make-A-Wish

Mary Alice Stephenson is the National Fashion Ambassador for Make-A-Wish® and will use GLAM4GOOD to grant fashion wishes for wish kids. As the event's host, stylist and emcee, Mary Alice will provide glamorous transformations leading up to a fashion photo shoot.

May 4, 2014 • Grace Centers of Hope

Grace Centers of Hope is the largest and oldest faith-based outreach to homeless and disadvantaged individuals and families. Located in Pontiac, Michigan, GHC proves a full recovery and rehabilitation campus for homeless men, women and children who have been abused or battle addiction. For its fifth season, GLAM4GOOD returns to the center to empower and pamper the women at GCH by featuring them in a fashion show.

May 31, 2014 • GLAM4GOOD Prom

Mary Alice Stephenson with the help of PEACE IS A LIFESTYLE will dress deserving teens from New York Public Schools for their high school proms. Students will experience a Touch of Glam as GLAM4GOOD and Suave pamper them and style their perfect prom look.
