About Mary Alice Stephenson

Mary Alice Stephenson is a world renowned style and beauty expert, humanitarian and the founder of GLAM4GOOD. She launched GLAM4GOOD in 2012 after serving a ten year tenure as the National Fashion Ambassador for Make-A-Wish® Foundation. Through GLAM4GOOD’s partnerships with non-profits and beauty brands, she has been able to provide life changing makeovers, dramatic giveaways and transformative experiences for everyday heroes.

GLAM4GOOD is on a mission to ignite positive change through style and transform lives through confidence-boosting makeovers, pampering the people who need it most. Check out the photos from past events and find one near you!

For more information on getting involved with GLAM4GOOD, please visit their website!

Upcoming Events

April 2014 • Make-A-Wish

Mary Alice Stephenson is the National Fashion Ambassador for Make-A-Wish, and will use GLAM4GOOD to pamper Make-A-Wish kids and their moms. As the event’s host, stylist and emcee, Mary Alice will provide glamorous transformations, leading up to on-stage makeover reveals.

May 4, 2014 • Grace Centers of Hope

Grace Centers of Hope is the largest and oldest homeless shelter in Detroit/Pontiac Michigan, which caters to over 34,000 children, women suffering from abuse, individuals challenged with mental illness and those whose loved ones suffer from addiction. On one Saturday each May, the residents leave their worries behind and enter a fantasy world created by Mary Alice Stephenson and GLAM4GOOD. This event will include fashion show collaboration with a world renowned designer and will feature women from the center to walk the runway.

May 31, 2014 • NY Public School Prom at the MET

Mary Alice Stephenson will dress deserving teens for prom after they experience the costume institute’s new Charles James exhibit at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in NYC. Student guests will experience a Touch of Glam as GLAM4GOOD and Suave pampers them and styles their perfect prom look, while teaching them how to be creative and artistic with fashion.

November 5, 2014 • STYLE4HEROES

For the third year in a row, Glam4Good worked with Bob and Lee Woodruff to create the fashion initiative STYLE4HEROS: a day of fashion and beauty that pampers the deserving from across the country to attend the annual gala. This event caters to female caretakers of wounded warriors, including some on active duty, and includes two days of red carpet treatment in preparation for the main event at Madison Square Garden. In the past, this event has featured top stars including Bruce Springsteen, Jerry Seinfeld, Jon Stewart and Robin Williams.
